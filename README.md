# Workflow Description

Workflow chosen:
Customer support email drafting

User:
A customer support representative who needs to respond to customer inquiries efficiently and consistently.

Input:
The system receives a customer message along with relevant company policy or context (e.g., refund policy, shipping rules).

Output:
A clear, polite, and professional draft email response that addresses the customer’s issue, follows company policy, and suggests next steps.

Why this task is valuable:
Customer support responses are repetitive and time-sensitive, making them well-suited for partial automation. This system can help reduce response time, improve consistency in tone and policy adherence, and allow human agents to focus on more complex or sensitive cases.
